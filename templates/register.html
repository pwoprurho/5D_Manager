Welcome to the Vinicius Command training manual. This guide provides a visual and technical walkthrough of the core operational workflows required to manage high-fidelity infrastructure projects across our international portfolio.

01

Unified Authentication Protocol

GOAL: SECURE HUB ACCESS
  1. Navigate to the high-contrast **Authentication Terminal**.
  2. Enter your corporate credentials (Email and Access Key).
  3. Click **AUTHENTICATE** to initialize your secure session.

*Note: Multi-factor authentication is active on all nodes.*

02

Personnel Enrollment & Registry

GOAL: STAFF DIRECTORY CONTROL
  • Access the **Admin: Personnel Registry** module.
  • Review active staff for role allocations.
  • Use **ENROLL STAFF** for new acquisitions.
  • Assign **Operational Roles** carefully.
03

Project Initialization & Ingest

GOAL: ANCHOR NEW ASSETS
  1. From **Projects**, click **INIT PROJECT**.
  2. Provide high-density documentation (PDF/BIM).
  3. Select a **Lead Facilitator** (Project Manager).
  4. Assign initial work packages via the task modal.
04

Site Modeling & Phase Creation

GOAL: DEFINE STRUCTURAL TIMELINE
  • Open **Site Manager** for your project.
  • Click **NEW PHASE** for structural milestones.
  • Set the **Operational Window** dates.
  • Phases auto-populate the Schedule and Kanban.
05

Strategic Kanban Orchestration

GOAL: REAL-TIME TELEMETRY MONITORING
  1. Navigate to the **Global Work Board**.
  2. Tasks categorized by: Not Started, In Progress, Verified.
  3. Drag cards to update telemetry from the field.
  4. Priority tasks pulse in UI for quick intervention.
06

Analytics HUD & Auditing

GOAL: COST & PROGRESS EXTRACTION
  • Access the **Analytics HUD** for bird's-eye view.
  • Monitor **Cost vs. Progress** variances.
  • Click **GENERATE REPORT** for formal audits.
  • System compiles field data into high-fidelity PDF.

Operational Compliance Directive

All field telemetry, including architectural site captures and manpower logs, must be synchronized daily. Delayed synchronization compromises the integrity of the Analytics HUD and budget actualization telemetry.
